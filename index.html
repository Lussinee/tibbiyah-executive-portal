03 | Positioning Target

The Ecosystem Orchestrator Blueprint

Recommended Play

The 10 / 20 / 70 Positioning Mix

Tibbiyah is not abandoning distribution or software integration; rather, it is reclassifying them as supporting operational functions of a larger, managed-service transformation engine.

10

Digital Health Distributor (10% emphasis): Serves as our initial entryway, tender engine, and foundational operational cash model.

20

Developer & Integrator (20% emphasis): Builds integration pipelines connecting disaggregated client systems.

70

Transformation Partner & Orchestrator (70% emphasis): Combines hardware, software, clinical pipelines, and education into premium recurring contracts.

KSA Operational Reference Alliance Medical Group

Managed Radiology PPP Model

Altakassusi Alliance Medical is a partnership of KFSH&RC, GE Healthcare, and Alliance Medical. They manage large-scale radiology centres, providing equipment, molecular services, staff support, and structured imaging academies across MOH networks.

PPP Model: Comprehensive outsourcing for public systems
Build + Operate: Financing, building, operating custom hubs
Staff Strategy: Training Academy pathways
Economics: 10-Year, 1.5M patients/year framework
Inspiration: Transition from selling imaging hardware to managed service contracts.
Global Corporate Reference Medtronic Care Journey

Medtronic: Hardware to Connected Care

Medtronic transitioned from a hardware manufacturer to an integrated care player in diabetes by acquiring Diabeter in 2015. They built remote data monitoring dashboards that linked sensors directly with algorithm-enabled treatment pipelines.

1. Diabeter Acquisition: From medical device sales to holistic diabetes outpatient clinics.
2. Connected Dashboard Services: Transitioning from device sales to risk management.
3. Agile MiniMed IPO (2026): Spin-off raising $560M for dedicated execution.
Inspiration: Lock-in values by wrapping device distribution inside remote clinical dashboards.
